CCPA privacy rights (not to sell my personal information)

Under the CCPA, among other rights, Californian consumers have the right

Demand that the company that collects the consumer's personal data disclose the categories and specific parts of the personal data that the company has collected about consumers.

Demand that the company delete any personal data the company has collected about the consumer.

Require that the company that sells the consumer's personal data does not sell the consumer's personal data.

If you make an enquiry, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.
