How many days after starting work can I count on first applications?

In the first month you will receive applications, but keep in mind that preparation It takes 1 week to launch an advertisement, then 1 week is training advertising campaigns and only in 3-4 weeks there will be stability in flow of new applications.

How many hypotheses can you test in the first month? work?

In the first month of work, a specific action plan is developed, namely, a road map of cooperation, we prescribe specific plans and actions. We test up to 3 products simultaneously, for each of which has an average of 3-4 hypotheses. If something is not worked - turn it off and test the next one, and so on until let's get to work!

How to work with your team if we have a big difference in time?

We work with European and Asian countries with a difference of up to 4 - 5 o'clock. The team is always in touch from 9:00 to 18:00 Kyiv time time. In this case, we always have 3 - 4 hours to meet with you during working hours and agree on a list of tasks.

What guarantees does the agency give regarding the number of leads in the first month? work?

We do not guarantee the number of applications in the first month of work for a new business that has not yet worked with Internet marketing. In the first month we will be able to identify the presence your target audience on the Internet and determine sales volume, which you can receive. For a business that already has a system works and has end-to-end analytics configured - such guarantees are provided!
