2. What is Be Budget

Be Budget helps you reach short-term and long-term financial goals and helps you manage and control your finances. Get a real-time picture of your financial state and spending habits, so you can save more and create good short-term and long-term financial goals. All you need to do is manually import your daily transactions and financial details, without any links to your bank accounts, and you’ll be able to track and manage your money using the tools and features available through our planner. We offer tips and recommendations that can help you save more and make smarter financial decisions. There is even an option to receive alerts on your due dates, so you can pay your bills on time. Avoid splurges and create clear and concise financial reports faster than ever before. All the tools in our planner are placed on a single dashboard that anyone can use.
