How to Keep Your Trading Account Secure

  • Tom Chen
  • General Trading


Online security is becoming more and more of a priority for businesses and individuals. When it comes to your trading accounts, security, privacy and being aware of what data you share online are crucial.

According to CNBC, there was 61% increase in phishing attacks in 2022 compared to the previous year and we expect 2023 to see an even bigger increase. Phishing is one of the most sophisticated ways scammers can access your online accounts, social media, banking and trading accounts. Always be suspicious of any calls, emails or messages you get, and never click links or share personal details.

We share this security guide, so you’ll know how to use the best security tactics possible to play your part to keep your Switch Markets account secure.

Set a Unique, Strong Password

Setting a strong password for other online platforms is critical to protect your Switch Markets’ data and personal information. Although we at Switch Markets keep your information safe and secure, other sites and online platforms may not protect your data as they should. As hackers know most people use the same password for multiple accounts, they often use the stolen password from one site to access other accounts owned by the victim.

To protect your Switch Markets account from any suspicious activity, here’s what you can do:

  • Use a unique password for every site and service. By doing so, you ensure that your other accounts will stay safe and secure even if hackers might steal one of your passwords. 
  • Use a strong password with at least 8 characters, including uppercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Remember, when you set a password, the more, the better. If you struggle with memorizing your passwords, you better use password manager software to keep your passwords safe. 
  • Change your passwords every 90 days.

Beware of Phishing Scams

Occasionally, hackers may try to contact Switch Markets clients to steal their data by pretending to be Switch Markets employees. If this occurs and a Switch Markets employee contacts you, please keep in mind that:

  • If you are not sure about the identity of the caller, contact us via our 24/7 live chat and confirm with our agents if the person who contacted you is, indeed, a member of the Switch Markets team.
  • We at Switch Markets will never provide any trading advice or guide you on how to invest your money.
  • As part of our security policy, we will not ask you to share any private data over our social media channels or directly through email. This includes payment details, ID, phone number, a copy of your ID or passport, and any sensitive information about your account. 
  • In case of need, we will only use our authorized channels to be informed about any sensitive information. This includes our ticketing system, live chat support, and WhatsApp channel. 
  • Be aware of any suspicious email activity from Switch Markets. Hackers may use this technique by sending emails and offering unrealistic claims and bonuses. If you have received a suspicious email from Switch Markets, please avoid clicking any link attached to the email.

Use VPN in Public Places

Hackers know where and when to attack. For instance, they use a technique known as war driving, a very common hacking method in which hackers drive in different areas in search of open Wi-Fi networks.

If you access your Switch Markets account using a publicly accessible Wi-Fi network, you need to secure your account from potential attacks. To do so, ensure that you:

  • Use a VPN service, and enable it when using your account in a public Wi-Fi network.
  • Always log out properly after using your account.

Lost or Stolen Device

If your device has been lost or stolen, inform us immediately so our security team can block the stolen device and protect your account.

Know Our Email

Emails are always sent to our clients from [email protected]. If you receive an email from Switch Markets with another email address, please ignore the email and notify us immediately by opening a ticket or contacting one of our representatives via our live chat service.

Let Us Worry About Security for You

We understand that adding security tools may not be at the top of your to-do list. But our goal is to increase security and ensure you are safe and secure when you are connected to Switch Markets trading systems!

That is why you can always contact our security experts, who will help you get the security and privacy tools needed to ensure you are safe from hacking and phishing attacks.

If you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to contact our support team members. We’re available around the clock and always happy to help.

We wish you a safe trading experience, Switch Markets security team.
