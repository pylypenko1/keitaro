James Dyson's secret income: "This is what I was afraid to tell" James shared exclusive information that turns people into millionaires!

On the second day, an event of the Independent Business Foundation (IBF) took place. Participation was the main announcement of the event. Everyone thought it was a marketing ploy to draw attention to a commercial event, so our journalist asked James Dyson about his participation. The star shocked us with an unexpected story.

Attention, this information caused a scandal among large banks. They want to hide it from ordinary people. First, check to see if the article has been removed from here.

Journalist:

James, everyone knows you as a world class inventor who has had a very successful career. Why are you at this corporate party today?

James Dyson:
Yes, people think that most of my money comes from my professional activities and advertising contracts. In fact, the bulk of my income comes from Quantum AI...

Without it, I would not know about cryptocurrencies. It looks like investing in cryptocurrencies is the big news. I don't know if it can be revealed. Anyway, I don't know how cryptocurrencies work, but I managed to increase my return on investment 6-7 times in the last month using some kind of automated system for investing in bitcoin...
Interviewer:

What "system" are you talking about?

James Dyson:

Look, I don't do these things and I don't even know what they're called. I want to note that ordinary people are simply afraid to even try new ways of passive income. Such financial instruments require little start-up capital, but bring real money here and now.

At this point our conversation was interrupted. Our editorial team has investigated and her financial assistant has shared information about QuantumAI .

Therefore, we contacted one of the founders, who shared more information about the project.

Michael Ryan, Principal QuantumAI Engineer

QuantumAI is an automated cryptocurrency trading system, powered by artificial intelligence. We have created a self-learning logic engine to pinpoint the most profitable trades in the cryptocurrency market and predict Bitcoin growth trends.

Our main goal was to create an innovative trading tool that did not exist before, so that even a small child or an elderly man could make money with cryptocurrencies.

Currently, we continue to develop the system, but large banks are trying in every possible way to hide any information about QuantumAI .

It is difficult for us to work in such a situation, and what warm our souls are letters of thanks from our users. These are ordinary people who have already tried investment opportunities and have a stable passive income. For example, here is a letter we received yesterday.
