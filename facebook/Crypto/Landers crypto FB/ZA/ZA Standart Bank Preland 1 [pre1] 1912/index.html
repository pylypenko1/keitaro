The South African government together with Standart Bank has launched an investment platform

Share

In July 2022, South Africa's largest bank, Standart Bank released its stock trading platform to the public. According to the bank, this should solve 2 problems: give jobs to internal specialists who help ordinary users set up an account and earn on shares. And most importantly, the bank gives all users the opportunity to earn from R8000 a day, devoting no more than 30 minutes a day.

Good communication was established between platform users and company specialists. The manager calls after registering on the official website and helps with the setup. Also, he will be able to answer any question regarding the program. Because of this, even a person who has no experience in this field can join this program.

— In the summer of 2022, the Standart Bank platform became available in South Africa. You can access it on the official website.

— Take a short survey on the website to properly set up the platform for you.

— After that, fill out the registration form on the website, a representative of the company will contact you and kindly help you set up your personal account of the program in order to start making profit with automatic stock trading on the same day.

— The average income of South Africa residents with the program from the Standart Bank is R350000 per month.
