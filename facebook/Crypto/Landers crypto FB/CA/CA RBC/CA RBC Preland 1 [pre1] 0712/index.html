    We were thrilled when more than 2500 clients started using the new platform from Royal Bank of Canada and are already earning 500-600 CAD per day. We can say with confidence that, thanks to the program we will be able to provide an easy passage through the global crisis for each user.

    In the summer of 2022, the Royal Bank of Canada platform became available in Canada. You can access it on the official website.
