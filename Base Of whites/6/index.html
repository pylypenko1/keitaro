Inbound Marketing

Inbound marketing is another term that refers to how you can use various online marketing channels to attract new customers. Basically, inbound digital marketing works through the process of creating content and "pushing" interested users through the sales funnel until they convert into leads or customers.

The main elements of inbound marketing are content, attention, engagement, trust, and satisfaction.

01.

The top of the funnel or the first step

Awareness. At this point, you can use Facebook ads to introduce your brand to Facebook users. Your goal is to attract the attention of as many people as possible and attract new subscribers.

02.

Second step

This consideration. At this stage, your goal is to get people (who have shown interest in your brand) and get them through the middle of the funnel to visit your website, install the app, send you a message, etc.

03.

Third step

This is a conversion. This is the final step when you convince people who enter your conversion funnel. Conversion can be anything that matters to your business, such as selling a product or service, calling or registering on your website.

Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is one of the oldest forms of marketing that produces great results when used online. The essence of affiliate marketing is that one company advertises another company's product and gets a commission every time it brings in a new customer.
