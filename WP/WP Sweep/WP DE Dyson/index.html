                    How to Choose the Best Upright Vacuum Cleaner

                    When it comes to choosing the best upright vacuum cleaner, there are several traits that separate them from the good ones. The addition of several features can be the difference between a clean home and a dirty one. With that said, these are the most important factors to consider:

                    1. Strong Cleaning Performance

                    A strong cleaning performance is arguably one of, if not the most, important factors when choosing the best upright vacuum cleaner. The most crucial factor is airflow, which comes from a powerful motor that creates a lot of suction, combined with an efficient intake path. Suction power is important too.

                    Higher suction power allows the vacuum to pick up heavier particles, like glass shards or sand. However, suction alone is not very effective at cleaning thick surfaces, so you need a brush roll to agitate something thick, like carpet fibers. The above features combine to deliver strong cleaning performance.

                    2. Filtration Effectiveness

                    Equally important with cleaning performance is filtration effectiveness. While some would argue that HEPA filters are not necessary, we beg to differ. A HEPA filtration system can remove 99.97 percent or more of airborne particles of 0.3 micron in diameter and can be useful for people with severe allergies and asthma.

                    It’s important to note, however, that the rest of the vacuum cleaner needs to be tightly sealed, too. If the gaskets around the HEPA filter aren’t perfectly tight, the filter itself is pointless. You don’t have to worry about controlling particle emissions, as our picks are excellent at keeping harmful particles at bay.

                    3. Corded Vacuum Model

                    For this best upright vacuum cleaners list, we’ve decided to go for corded models only. While the portable design of cordless vacuums makes them convenient, their small dust capacity and the battery limitations may not be suitable for all homes. Based on pure performance, corded vacuums are simply better.   

                    Additionally, we've found only a few cordless models that can match the cleaning power of corded vacuum cleaners. The best plug-in vacuum cleaners are almost always stronger cleaners than most other vacuum types. Best of all, corded vacuums have unlimited run time, perfect for homes of all sizes.

                    4. Excellent Durability

                    Last but not least, it’s important to invest in a vacuum cleaner made by reputable brands. We favored longer warranties, as well as brands with good customer service and products known for having a long lifespan. After all, you wouldn’t want to see your investment break down after a month or two now, would you?

                    The hallmark of an excellent product lies in its durability. There are only a handful of brands that are known to produce long-lasting vacuum cleaners, and we’ve included only the best products from so many available. Rest assured, you can have peace of mind knowing that your investment will not disappoint you.
