Flexible development for Android by SCRUM

Creation of programs for android do according to the methodology of flexible development of Scrum.


What does it mean?

-We form the complete list of tasks (the so-called «back-log»)
-We divide it into small sections (called "sprints"). We have a sprint last 1 week
-Step by step we program and demonstrate the result after each sprint


What does it give?

-You control which functional is now programmed
-You can change the priority of tasks
-After each sprint you see the result of the work. You can "touch" the application
